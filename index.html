Enter a goal and choose a priority to organize your goals by whatever criteria you like.

Click the pencil to edit your goal and the trash bin to delete it. You can delete completed goals and keep the points.

Upon completion, click the checkmark beside your goal. You get one point for every goal you complete.

You can then use these points to bet on the success of new goals of the same priority.

If you complete the new goal, you'll still get your one point, plus double your wager.

The point is to help you quantify the momentum you gain from completing goals and reinvest that mementum into new ones.

This site will store your data if you leave and come back, but resetting it will erase all your goals and points forever.
